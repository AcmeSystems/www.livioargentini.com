Master equalizer LA117

Multirange equalizer expecially designed for mastering and mixing use

Inside of LA 117 there are two different equalizers, the first section is a Baxandall type and the second section is a special 5 band parametric unit.
The parametric unit is based on a special innovative technology designed by Livio Argentini.
Instead of the other parametric equalizer LA 117 use a parallel configuration and this allowed a very low distorsion, noise end specially low phase rotation.

8 channels line pre Octopus LA88

“LED Technology” Saturation

Octopus LA-88 is an 8 channels line preamplifier designed for professional use.
It has 8 inputs and 8 separate outputs on the rear panel and 8 auxiliary inputs on the front panel. In addition to the transparency of the response and the quality of the circuit, the unique character of the Octopus derives from the possibility to have the first four channels (1> 4) linear and the last four channels (5> 8) colored, generating harmonics in the input signal through a newly designed “LED Technology” saturation circuit by Livio Argentini.
